ZEN AI Literacy Initiative 2024

Empowering the Next Generation of AI Innovators

Join the Revolution

The ZEN AI Literacy Initiative 2024 is a groundbreaking program, the first of its kind in US history. In partnership with the Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Washington (BGCGW) through their innovative Clubhouse@YourHouse program, we're bringing cutting-edge AI education to youth across DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

This 26-week intensive course, divided into 14 comprehensive modules, is designed to equip teenagers with the skills, knowledge, and ethical understanding needed to thrive in an AI-driven future.

Module 1: AI Foundations (Weeks 1-2)

  • Introduction to AI and its impact
  • Setting up your AI toolbox
  • Hands-on: Create your first AI program

Module 2: Python for AI (Weeks 3-4)

  • Python essentials for AI development
  • Data structures and algorithms
  • Project: Build a simple AI-powered application

Module 3: Machine Learning Basics (Weeks 5-6)

  • Supervised and unsupervised learning
  • Training and evaluating models
  • Workshop: Implement a basic ML algorithm

Module 4: Natural Language Processing (Weeks 7-8)

  • Text processing and analysis
  • Sentiment analysis and text generation
  • Project: Create an AI-powered chatbot

Module 5: Computer Vision (Weeks 9-10)

  • Image processing techniques
  • Object detection and facial recognition
  • Challenge: Develop an image classification system

Module 6: Deep Learning (Weeks 11-12)

  • Neural networks architecture
  • Training deep learning models
  • Project: Build a deep learning model for real-world data

Module 7: Reinforcement Learning (Weeks 13-14)

  • Basics of reinforcement learning
  • Q-learning and policy gradients
  • Workshop: Train an AI agent to play a simple game

Module 8: AI Ethics and Bias (Weeks 15-16)

  • Ethical considerations in AI development
  • Identifying and mitigating bias in AI systems
  • Debate: Crafting an AI Ethics Manifesto

Module 9: AI in Healthcare (Weeks 17-18)

  • AI applications in medical diagnosis
  • Predictive healthcare analytics
  • Case study: Design an AI solution for a healthcare challenge

Module 10: AI in Finance (Weeks 19-20)

  • AI-driven financial analysis
  • Algorithmic trading basics
  • Project: Develop a simple stock prediction model

Module 11: AI in Creative Industries (Weeks 21-22)

  • Generative AI for art and music
  • AI in content creation and design
  • Workshop: Collaborate with AI on a creative piece

Module 12: AI and Robotics (Weeks 23-24)

  • Fundamentals of robotics and AI integration
  • Sensor data processing and decision making
  • Challenge: Program a simple robot using AI techniques

Module 13: AI in Environmental Science (Week 25)

  • AI applications in climate modeling
  • Environmental data analysis using AI
  • Project: Develop an AI model for environmental prediction

Module 14: Future of AI and Career Pathways (Week 26)

  • Emerging AI technologies and trends
  • AI career opportunities and pathways
  • Capstone: Design your AI-enhanced future project

In partnership with the Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Washington, our Clubhouse@YourHouse program brings the AI learning experience directly to you. This innovative approach combines:

  • Virtual live sessions with expert instructors
  • Interactive online modules for self-paced learning
  • Virtual mentorship from industry professionals
  • Online collaborative projects with peers
  • Access to cutting-edge AI tools and platforms
  • Regular virtual meetups and networking events

Clubhouse@YourHouse ensures that you can access world-class AI education from the comfort of your home, while still enjoying the benefits of a collaborative, immersive learning experience.
